TGM-style Tetris game

Playfield: 10x20 squares
Pieces:

	      X      X  XX   XX   X   XX
	XXXX  XXX  XXX  XX  XX   XXX   XX

Game Speed: 62,5 ticks/second (16 ms)
Default Gravity: 32 ticks (~2 lines/second)
Inputs QWERTY-Keyboard:

	E - 1 col left
	F - 1 col right
	D - drop

	J - turn left
	I - turn right
	L - turn left

Grid Layout:

	1 box = 10x10 pixels
	11 vertical lines: (10 * 10) + 11 = 111
	21 horizontal lines: (20 * 10) + 21 = 221
